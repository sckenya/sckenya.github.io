How You can Get Involved

Being in charge of caring for children, we rely on monthly donations to provide for the daily life of the children in our home. As in any home, we have monthly expenses, including food purchases, education, hygiene, maintenance of the home, transport, and staff salaries. To ensure the monthly availability of the necessary funds we look for permanent sponsors, who agree to give a fixed amount every month and secure the survival of a child. These sponsors allow us to balance monthly income with monthly expenses and ensure that we can continue hosting the children. Godfathers and godmothers are the backbone of the Second Chance home.

On a monthly basis we currently spend about 35€ (45$) per child per month for food, hygiene, medical care and schooling. Yes, with about  one euro per day ($1.50)!  Added to this are the fixed monthly cost for running the orphanage, which amount to about 1200€ per month ($1500). They cover for all salaries, maintenance, transport, office materials, etc.

We are busy at Second Chance Orphanage creating the best possible environment for our children. There are many ways for you to be involved in the ongoing work of Second Chance. From the outset of our work, the overall purpose has been to improve the quality and standard of life of ALL of the children who live in the Home..

We wish to welcome more orphans to the home. We have a capacity to house thirty, and there is a long waiting list. What is needed is further funding to give us the confidence that we can sustain the bigger number beyond the current twenty.

Organic Farming

In Second Chance Home, people are hard working. With added farming knowledge and skills, they could produce higher value-add agricultural products, both for consumption and for sale. If advisory services in agriculture were made available, and cooperation among farmers significantly increased, such increased production from the fertile land would be possible. We have already partnered with experts to expand crops and identify markets, so that the profits from them can be returned to and reinvested in the orphanage.

To achieve this we plan to lease a land of approximately two acres. With this land we will be able to plant vegetables, onions and maize. Our goal is to raise USD 3000$.

Partner With Us

Already, we've had a number of volunteers express interest in travelling to Kenya to work with the community members in Second Chance. We are very excited about the possibilities of partnerships and "volunteerism," but we do not yet have an established process for taking advantage of these resources. We do know that we’d like to match potential volunteers with ongoing projects in Second Chance, and we vet all proposed volunteer visits through the dedicated people in the village who know how best to match resources to existing needs. If YOU are interested in traveling to Second Chance to see how you can help, please contact us.
