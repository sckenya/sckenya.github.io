Second Chance has been a community based organization registered under the ministry of social services in Kenya since 2008.

Our God-given vision is to reach street children and youth who are orphans and need physical help within the city of Kitale, as well as in the rural villages.

We want to take them in, help them build confidence and maturity, and teach them to be active members of their society. We teach them commitment, relationship with God, honesty, and accountability.

Who We Are

Second Chance Home is a residence for children who have lost their parents to AIDS, election violence and tribal clashes. Our home is located in the Kipsongo Slum of Kitale, in Kenya, Africa. Twenty children, ranging from ages 3 to 13 currently live at our Home, thanks to friends and well-wishers across the globe.

At Second Chance Orphanage we are committed to providing a loving home for our children, where they find safety, health and a new family. Our children attend the local pre- and primary schools, and we strive at our Home to be an integral part of our local village. Second Chance is a place where hope, love and determination has a place to grow.

Here you see the children happy and growing, in spite of the many challenges they each have faced. There is so much we are doing at Second Chance, but there is more to do! Please partner with us to help us grow and thrive.
